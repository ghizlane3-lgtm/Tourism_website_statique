Ifrane – The Little Switzerland of Morocco

Nestled in the heart of the Middle Atlas mountains, Ifrane is a peaceful escape known for its snowy winters, pristine parks, and charming European-style architecture. Stroll through cedar forests, enjoy the crisp mountain air, and explore scenic lakes and waterfalls that make Ifrane a true hidden gem.
