Chefchaouen – The Blue Pearl


Known as the Blue Pearl of Morocco, Chefchaouen enchants visitors with its blue-washed streets, charming medina, and stunning mountain backdrop. Wander through narrow alleys lined with artisan shops, taste local delicacies, and enjoy panoramic viewpoints that capture the essence of Morocco’s Rif region.
